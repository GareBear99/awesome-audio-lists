**Cover art**: re-use the pack's repo banner or generate a matching one.

**Tags**: 3–5 tags matching genre + pack theme (e.g., `synthwave`, `darkwave`, `samples`, `loops`, `free samples`).

**Download file**: upload the pack's latest GitHub-Release ZIP.
